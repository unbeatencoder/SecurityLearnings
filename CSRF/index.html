CSRF Vulnerable Transfer Page

This page is intentionally insecure for learning. It performs a money transfer using a plain POST form and does not include any CSRF protection.

Transfer Funds






Why this is vulnerable

  • State-changing action is triggered by a simple form POST.
  • No anti-CSRF token is included or validated.
  • If the victim is logged in, browser cookies are sent automatically with the cross-site request.

Typical defenses: CSRF tokens, SameSite cookies, and Origin/Referer checks.
